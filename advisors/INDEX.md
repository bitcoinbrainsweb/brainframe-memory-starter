## How to add an advisor

1. Copy `advisors/_template.md` to `advisors/[category]/[slug].md`
2. Fill in the corpus and mandatory steps
3. Add a row to this index with `status: active`
4. Ask Claude: "ask [name] about X" or "run this through [name]"

## Categories

- **strategic** -- frameworks, positioning, long-game thinking
- **tactical** -- execution, offers, negotiation, copy
- **thinker** -- first principles, worldview, philosophy
- **finance** -- capital, risk, burn, fundability
